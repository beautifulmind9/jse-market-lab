# Marketing Content Support

## Purpose
Use dashboard outputs to create simple, public-facing content that helps explain the product to everyday Jamaican investors and broader audiences.

## Typical outputs needed
- 3–5 key insights in simple language
- Anything surprising or unexpected
- Common mistakes the data reveals
- A short explanation of one feature and why it exists

## Primary use cases
- LinkedIn posts
- Instagram/TikTok captions or talking points
- Public dashboard explainers
- Product updates
- Case study storytelling
- Soft educational content for non-expert investors

## Important boundary
This content support layer is not automatically the same as in-app feature scope.

It uses dashboard outputs for communication and education, but may be produced outside the app itself.

## Content principles
- Use plain language
- Keep Jamaican investors in mind
- Avoid exaggerated certainty
- Avoid “signal-selling” tone
- Stay faithful to the actual dashboard outputs
- Focus on explanation, interpretation, and education

## Example prompt structure
“Using the latest dashboard outputs, give me:
- 3–5 key insights in simple language
- anything surprising or unexpected
- common mistakes the data reveals
- a short explanation of one feature and why it exists”

## Operational note
This file supports communication and marketing workflows, not necessarily the implementation backlog unless specific items are promoted into product scope.
