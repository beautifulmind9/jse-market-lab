# Sprint 2 — UAT Notes

## Objective
Validate whether decision guidance helps users understand how to respond to earnings-related risk.

## Scenarios
- High severity overlap
- Caution severity overlap
- Info severity overlap
- Null/unknown overlap
- Null severity fallback

## Questions to Validate
- Do users understand the guidance language?
- Do beginners understand terms like "reduce exposure" and "holding window"?
- Does the guidance feel helpful rather than controlling?
- Does the extra block improve clarity or create clutter?

## Early Observations
- Guidance improves the transition from risk awareness to action thinking
- Expander keeps the UI compact
- Further user testing is needed for wording clarity
