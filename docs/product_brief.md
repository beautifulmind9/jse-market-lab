# JSE Dashboard — Product Brief

## Problem
Retail investors in the Jamaican Stock Exchange (JSE) lack structured tools to evaluate trades. Most decisions are based on fragmented information, intuition, or delayed insights, with little consideration for trading costs, liquidity, or event risk (e.g. earnings).

## Target Users
- Beginner investors seeking guidance
- Intermediate/analyst users seeking structured decision support

## Product Goal
Provide a decision-support platform that helps users:
- evaluate trade opportunities
- understand risk factors (earnings, volatility, liquidity)
- compare holding windows
- account for real trading costs

## What This Product Is NOT
- Not a signal-selling service
- Not a prediction engine
- Not financial advice

## Success Criteria
- Users can evaluate a trade in a structured way
- Users understand risk before executing
- Users can compare opportunities objectively
- Decisions are made faster and with more clarity
